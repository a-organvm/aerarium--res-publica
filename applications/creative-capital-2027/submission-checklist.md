# Creative Capital 2027 — Submission Checklist

**Deadline:** April 2, 2026, 3:00 PM Eastern
**Method:** Web form at Creative Capital portal (desktop, Chrome/Firefox/Safari)
**Format:** ALL text fields are PLAIN TEXT. No markdown, no formatting.

---

## Pre-Submission

- [ ] Strip all markdown from draft.md before pasting into form
- [ ] Verify character counts one final time (limits include spaces)
- [ ] Prepare work sample (video or images) and upload/host
- [ ] Prepare resume PDF (art-only, 5+ years, concise)
- [ ] Have eligibility documents ready (US passport or equivalent)
- [ ] Use desktop browser

## Form Fields — Complete Inventory

### Eligibility Confirmation (checkboxes)
- [ ] US citizen/permanent resident/Tribal ID/O-1 visa
- [ ] At least 25 years old
- [ ] 5+ years professional artistic practice
- [ ] Not enrolled in degree program
- [ ] Not applying to Warhol Arts Writers Grant same year
- [ ] Never previously received Creative Capital Award
- [ ] Not on more than one proposed project

### Applicant Information
- [ ] Legal name
- [ ] Professional/chosen name (if different)
- [ ] Current city, state, zip, country
- [ ] Primary phone number
- [ ] Email address
- [ ] Date of birth
- [ ] Hometown (city, state, zip, country — geographic roots)
- [ ] Career stage: **Mid-career** (dropdown)
- [ ] Short bio (50 words) → `bio.md#short`
- [ ] Long bio (200 words) → `bio.md#long`
- [ ] Artist resume (PDF upload)
- [ ] Artist website (optional but recommended)

### Demographic Fields (not shared with reviewers)
- [ ] Gender identity and pronouns
- [ ] Race/ethnicity
- [ ] Tribal affiliation (if applicable)
- [ ] Disability identification

### Project Information
- [ ] Primary discipline: **Visual Arts** (dropdown)
- [ ] Sub-disciplines (up to 3): **Technology, Social Practice, Sound Art**
- [ ] Project title (10 words max) → `draft.md#title`
- [ ] One-line description (400 chars max) → `draft.md#one-line`

- [ ] Estimated completion: **December 2028** (month + year)

## Funds Disbursement (critical for institutional strategy)

- Funds go to **individual artist** or a 501(c)(3) they operate
- **CANNOT** be routed through LLCs or fiscal sponsors
- Must have US bank account
- Affects: IRF-INST-003 (Aspiration Tech), IRF-INST-004 (LLC formation) — Creative Capital is individual-only

## Round II (June 2026, by invitation only — DO NOT PREPARE NOW)

- Itemized project budget (PDF)
- Project timeline (1-page PDF)
- Up to 2 additional work samples
- Proof of eligibility (passport/birth certificate/etc.)
